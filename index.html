The method

How a Hunt Works

Three movements. No manual provided.

🕯
Find the trick

Every element on the page is a puzzle in disguise. A button that won't press, a field that won't accept text, a reflection that seems wrong. The mechanism is always there — waiting to be noticed by someone paying close attention.

🔍
Use it correctly

Once the trick reveals itself, you must operate it precisely. Drag the lantern to the exact spot. Hold the glass at the right angle. Type the sequence in the right order. Approximation will not do. Neither will guessing.

📜
Assemble the answer

Each solved element yields a fragment — a letter, a symbol, a number. The fragments combine into a final answer you submit to claim your place on the leaderboard. Speed matters. Paid clues cost you rank. Pride is its own reward.
