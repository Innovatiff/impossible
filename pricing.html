On the Matter of Purchased Clues

A clue is available at any point during any hunt, to any signed-in solver. Clues are purchased as credits — one credit reveals one clue. Credits never expire and carry across hunts.

A clue does not solve the puzzle for you. It narrows the territory — confirms a direction, eliminates a wrong path, hints at the mechanism without demonstrating it. The work remains yours.

⚠

Every clue you use is recorded. Your Detective Score on the leaderboard is calculated from time elapsed and clues used. Solving without a single clue is the highest demonstration of the craft. Paying to progress is always an option. Being remembered for it is not.

Questions of record

Frequently Raised

  • Purchased clues ($1.99 each) do not expire. Once bought, they remain available for use in the hunt you purchased them for, for as long as that hunt is active on the platform. They are not transferable between hunts. The free monthly clue credit available to Full Dossier members expires at the end of each billing cycle and does not carry forward to the next month.

  • Yes, at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain full access to all eight locked hunts until then. After cancellation, access to hunts covered only by membership is removed. Any hunt you purchased individually (at $3.99 per hunt) before or during your membership remains yours permanently, regardless of subscription status.

  • Yes, always. A hunt bought individually at $3.99 is a one-time purchase. It belongs to your account permanently. Subscribing to Full Dossier membership later does not change or affect that ownership, and cancelling the membership does not remove access to individually purchased hunts. The two are entirely independent ledger entries.

  • A clue is a clue. The leaderboard does not distinguish between a clue that cost you $1.99 and a clue that came with your membership. In both cases, the score penalty is identical. The free monthly credit is a benefit of membership — not a way around the rules of the game. Solve without clues and your Detective Score will reflect it. The record is honest.

  • Individual hunt purchases and clue purchases are non-refundable once the hunt has been accessed or the clue has been revealed. Membership charges for a billing period already in progress are similarly non-refundable, though you may cancel to prevent future charges at any time. If you encounter a technical error preventing access to content you have paid for, contact us — that is a different matter entirely.
