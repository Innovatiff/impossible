Hunt 02 · The Whispering Page

The Venturi Mystery

A Victorian parlour. A locked private room. And somewhere between them — the secret life of Signor Giacomo Venturi, an Italian man of letters who vanished without explanation.

He left two rooms and thirty clues. Each room contains objects that whisper — if you know how to listen. Some whispers become words. Six words, each beginning with a letter. Together, those six letters spell the answer.

YOUR OBJECTIVE

Explore both rooms. Solve each puzzle. Collect the six evidence words. Use their first letters to deduce the final answer — a single word that names what Venturi concealed his entire life.
