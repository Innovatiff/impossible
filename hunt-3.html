🔏 Classified — Hunt 03

You have been admitted to a room that has not been opened in eleven years.

The previous occupant left no note. No forwarding address. Only what you see before you: a chamber sealed from within, its contents undisturbed, its secrets still folded into the furniture like pressed flowers between pages.

Four digits comprise the lock on the door at the end of this room. You will not find them written in a single place. You will find them the way all things of value are found — through careful attention to what others dismissed as ornament.

The chamber remembers. Begin when you are ready.

— The Warden
